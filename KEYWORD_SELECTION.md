# Keyword Selection Decision

## Decision Status

Proceed with a TikTok Shop fee-focused calculator, not a generic profit calculator.

## Primary SEO Target

**tiktok shop fee calculator**

Why:

- Best early keyword signal from Ubersuggest screenshot: Volume 320, CPC $20.84, SEO Difficulty 12.
- Clear calculator intent.
- US seller commercial value.
- Competitor pages exist, confirming demand.

## Page Concept

**TikTok Shop Fee Calculator for US Sellers**

Angle:

> Calculate TikTok Shop fees and true profit after referral/platform fee, commission fee, creator affiliate commission, coupons, shipping subsidy, refunds, and ads.

This is fee-first, profit-second.

## Secondary Keywords To Cover On The Same Page

- tiktok shop fees calculator
- tiktok shop seller fees
- tiktok shop fees usa
- tiktok shop commission fee
- tiktok shop commission rate
- tiktok shop referral fee
- tiktok shop fee percentage
- how to calculate tiktok shop fees
- what fees does tiktok shop charge sellers

## Supporting Pages Later

1. `/tiktok-shop-commission-fee-calculator/`
   - Target: `tiktok shop commission fee`, `tiktok shop commission rate`
   - Purpose: calculate platform/referral fee and explain TikTok's terminology.

2. `/tiktok-shop-profit-calculator/`

3. `/tiktok-shop-fbt-calculator/`
   - Target: `tiktok shop fbt calculator`, `tiktok fbt`
   - Purpose: later page if FBT demand grows.

4. `/tiktok-shop-seller-fees/`
   - Target: `tiktok shop seller fees`, `tiktok shop fees for sellers`
   - Purpose: guide page with internal links to the calculator.

5. `/tiktok-shop-settlement-period/`
   - Target: `tiktok shop settlement period`, `tiktok shop settlement time`
   - Purpose: later informational page; weak volume but seller pain point.

## What The V1 Calculator Should Include

Inputs:

- Selling price
- Product cost
- TikTok Shop referral/platform fee
- Affiliate/creator commission
- Coupon or discount
- Seller shipping subsidy
- Shipping / fulfillment cost
- Ad spend per order
- Refund rate
- Monthly orders

Outputs:

- TikTok Shop fee amount
- Creator commission amount
- Total fees and selling costs
- Net revenue per order
- Net profit per order
- Profit margin
- Break-even ROAS
- Monthly profit estimate

## Differentiation Requirement

Most existing pages are either:

- Basic fee calculators
- Blog posts explaining seller fees
- Generic profit calculators

Our page should be positioned as:

> A practical TikTok Shop US seller margin calculator that starts with fees but answers the real seller question: can this product still make money after fees, creator commission, discounts, shipping, refunds, and ads?

## Stop / Continue Criteria

Continue if:

- The page can rank for the main keyword plus long-tail variants.
- SERP includes smaller tools/blogs, not only official or very large authority sites.
- The calculator can offer a better UX and more complete seller-cost model.
- The first version remains pure frontend: no login, no backend, no stored user data, no paid API dependency.
- Fee assumptions are editable by the user, reducing maintenance risk when TikTok updates policy.

Stop or pivot if:

- Search Console shows no impressions after 60-90 days with basic indexing and internal links.
- SERP becomes dominated by stronger authority sites with highly polished calculators.
- TikTok fee data becomes too difficult to keep current without official API/source access.
- The tool requires real-time policy, account, settlement, or marketplace data that cannot be represented safely with editable assumptions.

## Framework Note

This decision adapts the user's prior tool-site selection framework:

- Keep: calculator utility, pure frontend implementation, low maintenance, long-tail SEO, no-login usage.
- Adjust: do not require >1000 monthly searches for this first niche. In this market, a lower-volume keyword with high CPC and strong seller intent can be more valuable than a broad high-volume utility keyword.
