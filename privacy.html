Privacy

Privacy Policy

ShopFeeCalc is a browser-based calculator. The current version does not require an account, does not ask for personal information, and does not store calculator inputs on a server.

Calculator Data

Values entered into the calculator are processed in your browser to estimate fees, costs, margins, and related outputs. We do not receive or store those values in the current static version of the site.

Analytics and Advertising

ShopFeeCalc may use Google services, including Google AdSense and related measurement tools, to display ads, understand site usage, and help keep the calculator free. These services may use cookies or similar technologies to measure ad performance, prevent abuse, and personalize or limit ads depending on your settings and applicable law.

You can manage Google ad personalization in your Google account and browser settings. ShopFeeCalc does not sell calculator inputs or require an account to use the calculator.

Contact

For privacy questions or calculator feedback, contact us at contact@shopfeecalc.com.
