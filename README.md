# ShopMargin

Free TikTok Shop fee calculators for US sellers.

## Current MVP

- TikTok Shop Fee Calculator for US Sellers
- Calculates TikTok Shop fee amount, creator commission, total selling costs, net revenue, net profit, profit margin, break-even ROAS, and suggested target price
- Includes TikTok-specific assumptions:
  - TikTok Shop fee rate
  - Affiliate commission
  - Coupon or discount
  - Seller shipping subsidy
  - Ad spend
  - Refund rate

## Open Locally

Open `index.html` in a browser.
