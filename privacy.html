Privacy Policy

ShopMargin is a browser-based calculator. The current version does not require an account, does not ask for personal information, and does not store calculator inputs on a server.

Calculator Data

Values entered into the calculator are processed in your browser to estimate fees, costs, margins, and related outputs. We do not receive or store those values in the current static version of the site.
